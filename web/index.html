01

Grad-CAM

Gradient-weighted class activation maps from the last convolutional block. Three variants: standard, Grad-CAM++, and EigenCAM.

02

Temperature Scaling

Single scalar T learned on the validation set. ECE dropped from 0.105 → 0.030 — a 72% improvement in calibration.

03

MC Dropout

Dropout layers stay active at inference. 30 stochastic passes produce a predictive entropy score per image.

04

Two-Phase Training

Head warmup for 3 epochs, then full backbone fine-tune at lr/5 with CosineAnnealingLR. AUC jumped 0.876 → 0.915 at unfreezing.

About

Why calibration
matters more
than accuracy

Built end-to-end by Toumi Rayane

Most ML projects report accuracy and stop there. In medical AI, confidence calibration separates a research demo from something deployable.

A model that says "97% melanoma" when it should say "62%" causes harm. Temperature scaling is a one-line post-hoc fix with provable guarantees on the validation set.

Uncertainty quantification via MC Dropout means the model can say "I don't know" — flagging ambiguous cases for human review.
